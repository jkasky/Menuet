Use NSWorkspace notification center to watch of app launch/terminate/activate.

Use accessibility UI to read menu and to programmatically interact with menu.

Use AXUIElementCreateApplication to create top-level AXUIElement for app with
given pid. Traverse the element to find menu.

Use kAXMenuItemCmdCharAttribute and frieds to get cmd shortcut

Use AXUIElementPerformAction to invoke menu items.
